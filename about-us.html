About Us

Introduction

The Shelter for Urban Homeless (SUH) is dedicated to providing safe and secure housing for homeless individuals in urban areas. Our aim is to ensure that everyone has access to basic amenities and support services that can help them rebuild their lives.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide comprehensive support and resources to homeless individuals, ensuring their safe reintegration into society. We envision a society where no individual has to live on the streets, and everyone has access to safe shelter and essential services.

Objectives and Goals

  • Provide safe and supportive shelter for urban homeless people.
  • Offer access to medical care, mental health support, and rehabilitation services.
  • Facilitate pathways to permanent housing through counseling and assistance programs.

Advantages of Our Services

Our services are designed to offer:

  • A safe and stable environment for individuals experiencing homelessness.
  • Comprehensive healthcare and psychological support tailored to individual needs.
  • Assistance and advocacy in securing sustainable housing solutions.

Facilities at Our Shelters

  • Well-ventilated rooms.
  • Potable drinking water and sanitation.
  • Adequate bathing & toilet facilities.
  • Standard lighting and fire protection measures.
  • First aid kits and pest control.
  • Common kitchen with necessary utensils and gas connections.
  • Child care facilities linked with nearby Anganwadi Centers.
  • Personal lockers and common recreation space.

Our Approach

At SUH, we adopt a holistic approach to addressing homelessness. This includes immediate relief through shelter, long-term solutions through rehabilitation and housing programs, and continuous support through counseling and healthcare services. We work closely with local authorities, healthcare providers, and community organizations to ensure comprehensive care for our residents.

Registration Process

Registering with SUH is simple:

  • Visit our website and fill out the online registration form.
  • Visit one of our local offices to speak with a registration officer and complete the application process in person.

For more information or to get in touch with us, please visit our Contact Us page. You can also follow us on our social media channels for updates and news.
